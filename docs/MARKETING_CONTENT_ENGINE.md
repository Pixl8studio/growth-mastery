# Marketing Content Engine - User Guide

## Overview

The Marketing Content Engine is a comprehensive organic social content generation system
integrated into Step 12 of the Genie AI funnel builder. It generates platform-optimized,
story-driven posts in your authentic founder voice and publishes directly to Instagram,
Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Enable Marketing Engine

1. Navigate to Step 12 in your funnel builder
2. Toggle "Enable Marketing Content Engine"
3. A profile is auto-created with your brand voice from intake call and offer data

### Step 2: Connect Social Platforms

Go to **Settings** tab and connect your platforms:

- Click "Connect" for each platform
- Authorize access via OAuth
- Confirm connection status shows green checkmark

### Step 3: Calibrate Your Voice (Optional)

Go to **Profile** tab:

1. Paste 3-5 of your existing social posts in the "Sample Content" field
2. Click "Calibrate Voice"
3. Echo Mode analyzes your writing patterns
4. Review identified voice characteristics

### Step 4: Generate Your First Content

Go to **Generate** tab:

1. **Create Brief:**
   - Name: "Q1 Lead Gen Campaign"
   - Topic: Your content topic
   - Goal: Select from dropdown
   - Target Audience: Describe your ICP
   - Transformation Focus: What change do you help create?
   - Select platforms (check Instagram, Facebook, etc.)
   - Choose story framework preference

2. **Generate:**
   - Click "Generate Content"
   - AI creates 3 story angles (Founder, Myth-Buster, Industry POV)
   - Platform variants auto-generated for selected platforms

3. **Review & Edit:**
   - Review each platform variant
   - Edit copy inline if needed
   - Check preflight validation status
   - Ensure alt text is provided for images

4. **Publish or Schedule:**
   - Click "Publish Now" for immediate posting
   - Or click "Schedule" to add to calendar

## Best Practices

### Content Creation

1. **Start with Sandbox** - Test content before going live
2. **Use Echo Mode** - Maintains authentic voice consistency
3. **Leverage Trends** - Timely content performs better
4. **Test Frameworks** - Try different story angles to find what resonates
5. **Optimize for O/I-1000** - Focus on conversions, not vanity metrics

### Voice Calibration

- Provide at least 3-5 sample posts for accurate calibration
- Recalibrate monthly as your voice evolves
- Use posts that performed well and represent your authentic style

### Platform Strategy

- **Instagram**: Visual-first, conversational, emoji-friendly
- **Facebook**: Community-driven, personal stories
- **LinkedIn**: Professional insights, thought leadership
- **Twitter**: Brief, punchy, conversation starters

### Publishing Workflow

1. Generate content in Sandbox
2. Review and edit as needed
3. Run preflight validation (must pass)
4. Promote to Production
5. Schedule or publish immediately
6. Monitor analytics

## Compliance & Safety

### Automatic Checks

Every post runs through preflight validation:

- **Compliance**: Health, finance, legal disclaimers
- **Accessibility**: Alt text, reading level (Grade 8 max)
- **Brand Voice**: Echo Mode drift detection
- **Character Limits**: Platform-specific validation

### Industry Disclaimers

Automatically added for:

- Health claims or medical advice
- Financial recommendations
- Coaching testimonials
- Income/earnings statements

### Copyright

Only use:

- Your own original content
- Licensed stock images
- User-generated content with permission
- Properly attributed quotes

## Troubleshooting

### Content Not Generating

- Verify profile exists and is active
- Check that intake call and offer data are complete
- Ensure OpenAI API key is configured

### Publishing Fails

- Verify platform connection is active
- Check token hasn't expired (reconnect if needed)
- Ensure content passes preflight validation
- Review error message in calendar entry

### Preflight Validation Issues

- **Compliance**: Add disclaimers or soften claims
- **Accessibility**: Provide alt text for images
- **Brand Voice**: Content may be too far from your established voice
- **Character Limit**: Shorten content for platform requirements

### Platform Connection Issues

- Ensure correct OAuth scopes are granted
- Verify redirect URLs match environment
- Check platform API access hasn't been revoked
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
