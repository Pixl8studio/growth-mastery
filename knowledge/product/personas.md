# Growth Mastery - User Personas

## Primary Persona: Expert Coach/Consultant

### Sarah - The Established Coach

**Demographics**:

- Age: 38-52
- Role: Executive/Business Coach
- Experience: 10+ years in field
- Income: $150k-$500k/year
- Location: US/Canada/UK/Australia

**Situation**:

- Has proven methodology and client results
- Currently does 1-on-1 coaching ($5k-$25k packages)
- Wants to scale beyond trading time for money
- Knows webinars work but overwhelmed by execution
- Tried DIY funnel building - abandoned halfway through

**Goals**:

- Launch automated webinar to scale reach
- Convert cold traffic to $2k-$10k programs
- Reduce dependency on referral-only business
- Maintain quality while scaling

**Pain Points**:

- "I'm not a copywriter - I don't know how to write sales copy"
- "I tried ClickFunnels but got stuck creating the presentation"
- "Hiring a copywriter costs $10k and takes 8 weeks"
- "I have the expertise, I just need help packaging it"

**What They Need**:

- Fast path from expertise to funnel (days not months)
- AI that understands coaching/consulting language
- Professional output without hiring expensive help
- Simple enough for non-technical person

**Success Looks Like**:

- Published webinar funnel in under 1 week
- 20-30% registration â†’ show rate
- 5-10% enrollment conversion
- Automated follow-up handling objections
- $20k+ monthly revenue from funnel

## Secondary Persona: Course Creator

### Marcus - The Online Educator

**Demographics**:

- Age: 32-45
- Role: Course Creator/Educator
- Platform: Currently using Teachable/Kajabi
- Revenue: $50k-$200k/year
- Audience: 5k-50k email list

**Situation**:

- Has successful course ($500-$2k price point)
- Launches work but exhausting (live webinars every time)
- Wants evergreen automated system
- Comfortable with technology but not a developer

**Goals**:

- Convert live webinar to evergreen automated funnel
- Maintain conversion rates without being live
- Scale to multiple funnels for different offers
- Free up time for course creation

**Pain Points**:

- "Live launches are exhausting - I need evergreen"
- "EverWebinar is just video delivery - I still write everything"
- "Want professional funnel without developer costs"
- "Need it to integrate with my existing email/CRM"

**What They Need**:

- Quality comparable to their live presentations
- Webhook integration with existing CRM (ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign)
- Analytics to see what's working
- Fast iteration (test new angles quickly)

**Success Looks Like**:

- Evergreen funnel converting at 80% of live rate
- Multiple funnels for different course offerings
- Automated follow-up sequences
- 4-6 hours/week instead of 20+ hours for launches

- Role: Agency Owner/Marketing Director
- Clients: 10-50 small business clients

- Team: 5-20 people

## Anti-Persona: Who This ISN'T For

### The DIY Hobbyist

- Just starting out, no proven offer
- No budget for ads or tools
- Expects free tier to build business
- **Why not**: Needs validation first, not automation

### The Enterprise Corporation

- 50+ person marketing teams
- Complex approval workflows
- Custom development requirements
- **Why not**: Too opinionated, not enterprise-flexible enough

### The Affiliate Marketer

- Promoting other people's products
- High-volume low-quality traffic strategies
- Looking for quick money schemes
- **Why not**: Our AI creates expert content, not affiliate promos

### The "Just Page Builders"

- Already happy with Unbounce/Leadpages
- Just needs landing pages, not funnels
- No interest in presentations or webinars
- **Why not**: We're webinar-specific, not general page builder

## Persona Usage Guidelines

### For Product Decisions

When evaluating features, ask: "Does Sarah need this to launch successfully?"

If it's only relevant to Lisa (agency), it's Phase 2 or 3.

### For Marketing

Sarah's language: "Turn your expertise into automated revenue" Marcus's language:
"Evergreen webinar funnels that convert like your live launches" Lisa's language:
"Deliver client funnels in days, not weeks"

### For Support

Sarah needs: Hand-holding, clear tutorials, "how to" guidance Marcus needs: Integration
docs, technical specs, API access Lisa needs: Bulk operations, client management,
reporting
