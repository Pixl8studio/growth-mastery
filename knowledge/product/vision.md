# Growth Mastery - Product Vision

## What Is This?

Growth Mastery is an AI-powered webinar funnel builder that transforms expertise into
automated sales systems. Creators go from initial consultation call to complete,
published webinar funnel - all generated by AI and ready to convert.

## The Problem We Solve

**The Pain**: Expert coaches, consultants, and course creators know their stuff but
struggle to package it into high-converting webinar funnels. Traditional approaches
require:

- Weeks of manual work creating slides, scripts, and sales copy
- Hiring expensive copywriters ($3k-$10k)
- Technical skills to build registration pages, video hosting, payment processing
- CRM integration expertise for follow-up automation
- Trial-and-error to find what converts

**Result**: Most experts never launch their webinar, or launch something mediocre that
doesn't convert.

## Our Solution

Growth Mastery uses AI to generate the entire funnel from a single intake conversation:

**Input**: 15-minute AI voice call where creator describes their offer, audience, and
approach

**Output**: Complete webinar funnel with:

- 55-slide presentation deck (Gamma-powered, 20 themes)
- Professional talk track/script
- Watch page with video player and CTA
- Registration page optimized for conversions
- Enrollment/sales page with all the elements
- AI-powered follow-up sequence (5 segments, personalized)
- Contact management with webhook delivery to their CRM
- Analytics tracking across the entire funnel

**Timeline**: Hours instead of weeks. Quality that rivals $10k+ custom builds.
