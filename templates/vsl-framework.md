# VSL (Video Sales Letter) Framework

## 5-10 Slide Short Script for Sales Pages

This framework creates a concise, high-converting video sales letter designed to
generate immediate action. Perfect for sales pages where the pitch needs to be tight,
focused, and emotionally compelling.

---

## Slide 1: Hook - Grab Attention Immediately

**Section:** hook **Purpose:** Stop the scroll, capture attention with a bold promise or
intriguing question

**Content Strategy:**

- Open with a provocative question or bold statement
- Address the viewer's deepest desire or pain point directly
- Create curiosity gap that demands resolution
- Use pattern interrupt to break through noise

**Focus Areas:**

- What transformation are you offering?
- What's the one thing they desperately want?
- What false belief is holding them back?

---

## Slide 2: Problem - Agitate the Pain

**Section:** problem **Purpose:** Make them feel the problem viscerally, so solution
becomes irresistible

**Content Strategy:**

- Paint vivid picture of their current frustration
- Show them what they're losing every day they wait
- Connect problem to their identity and future
- Make cost of inaction crystal clear

**Focus Areas:**

- What keeps them up at night?
- What have they already tried that failed?
- What's the real cost of staying stuck?

---

## Slide 3: Solution Introduction - Your Unique Answer

**Section:** solution **Purpose:** Position your offer as THE solution they've been
searching for

**Content Strategy:**

- Reveal your unique mechanism or approach
- Differentiate from everything else they've tried
- Show why THIS works when everything else failed
- Build credibility with proof or authority

**Focus Areas:**

- What makes your solution different?
- Why does it work when others don't?
- What's your unique insight or methodology?

---

## Slide 4: Proof & Transformation - Show It Works

**Section:** solution **Purpose:** Provide irrefutable evidence that your solution
delivers results

**Content Strategy:**

- Share powerful testimonials or case studies
- Show before/after transformations
- Provide specific, quantifiable results
- Let success stories sell for you

**Focus Areas:**

- What results have others achieved?
- What transformation will they experience?
- How fast can they see results?

---

## Slide 5: The Offer - What They Get

**Section:** offer **Purpose:** Detail exactly what they receive and the value they're
getting

**Content Strategy:**

- Break down all components of the offer
- Stack value with bonuses and extras
- Show total value vs. actual price
- Create no-brainer decision

**Focus Areas:**

- What's included in the core offer?
- What bonuses add massive value?
- What's the total value they're receiving?

---

## Slide 6: Urgency & Scarcity - Create FOMO

**Section:** offer **Purpose:** Give them a reason to act NOW instead of later

**Content Strategy:**

- Limited-time pricing or bonuses
- Limited spots or quantities
- Countdown timer or deadline
- Show what they lose by waiting

**Focus Areas:**

- Why must they act today?
- What will they miss if they wait?
- Is there a deadline or limit?

---

## Slide 7: Risk Reversal - Remove All Resistance

**Section:** offer **Purpose:** Eliminate fear by removing all risk from the decision

**Content Strategy:**

- Strong money-back guarantee
- No-risk trial or pilot program
- You-win-either-way positioning
- Transfer all risk to you

**Focus Areas:**

- What guarantee removes their fear?
- How long do they have to decide?
- What happens if they're not satisfied?

---

## Slide 8: Call to Action - Tell Them Exactly What to Do

**Section:** close **Purpose:** Make taking action completely clear and frictionless

**Content Strategy:**

- Clear, specific CTA button/instructions
- Remove friction from purchase process
- Remind them of transformation waiting
- Final emotional appeal to action

**Focus Areas:**

- What's the exact next step?
- Is it clear how to get started?
- Have you removed all barriers?

---

## Slide 9: Final Warning - Last Chance Reminder (Optional)

**Section:** close **Purpose:** One last push for those still on the fence

**Content Strategy:**

- Remind of what they'll miss
- Reinforce urgency/scarcity
- Paint picture of regret vs. success
- End with emotional appeal

**Focus Areas:**

- What's at stake if they don't act?
- What will their future look like with vs. without this?

---

## Slide 10: P.S. - Bonus Reminder or FAQ (Optional)

**Section:** close **Purpose:** Address last-minute objections or remind of key benefit

**Content Strategy:**

- Answer common objections
- Remind of guarantee or bonus
- Reinforce uniqueness of offer
- Final CTA

**Focus Areas:**

- What final objection needs addressing?
- What key benefit deserves repeating?
- Is the CTA clear one more time?

---

## VSL Framework Summary

This 5-10 slide framework is designed for maximum conversion in minimum time. Each slide
builds on the last to create an irresistible emotional and logical case for immediate
action.

**Psychological Flow:** Hook (Attention) → Problem (Pain) → Solution (Hope) → Proof
(Belief) → Offer (Value) → Urgency (Action) → Risk Reversal (Safety) → CTA (Direction) →
Warning (Fear) → P.S. (Reinforcement)

The goal is to move viewers from curiosity to conviction to conversion as quickly and
powerfully as possible.
